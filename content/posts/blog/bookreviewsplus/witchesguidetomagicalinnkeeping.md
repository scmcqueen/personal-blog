# *A Witch's Guide to Magical Innkeeping* by Sangu Mandanna

An autumn breeze felt on your cheek while sitting on your deck, a spot of sunshine through a frosty window. *A Witch's Guide to Magical Innkeepin*g is these moments encapsulated in a book.

Sera lost her magic, because she brought back her deceased aunt Jasmine back to life. This un-regrettable decision is the reason why she lost her friends, her place in school, and her ability to do magic. What does she do now? Run the inn she grew up in, of course! Sera devotes her life to building a place of comfort, safety, and love. A place where her nephew can run wild, two old women can find love, a young man can don his armor in peace, and maybe a handsome professor can raise his high-needs younger sister.

I enjoyed *A Witch's Guide to Magical Innkeeping* the way I enjoy a cup of hot tea with honey. Sweet, tasty, and easily drinkable. I would recommend to all!
